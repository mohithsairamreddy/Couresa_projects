Tablets:

Tablet computer is a mobile computer with a display, circuitry and battery in one single unit. Tablets can be seen as a bigger version of mobiles, since they are equipped with cameras, microphone, accelerometer and touchscreen in which you can use stylus pen replacing computers mouse and keyboard.
